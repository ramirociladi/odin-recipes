Milanesa de Pollo

Description:

A popular dish similar to schnitzel, milanesa de pollo consists of breaded and fried chicken cutlets, usually served with mashed potatoes or a simple salad.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into thin cutlets
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Season the chicken cutlets with salt and pepper.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl. In another plate, mix breadcrumbs and Parmesan.
  3. Coat each cutlet in flour, dip it in the beaten eggs, and finally coat it with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and fry the cutlets until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
