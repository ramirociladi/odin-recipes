Empanadas de Carne

Description:

Empanadas are a staple in Argentina, commonly filled with beef, onions, spices, and sometimes eggs or olives. They're enjoyed as a snack or main dish, often accompanied by a glass of wine.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 100 g green olives, sliced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
