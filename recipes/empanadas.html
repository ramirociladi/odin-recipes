Empanadas de Carne

Description:

Empanadas are a staple in Argentina, commonly filled with beef, onions, spices, and sometimes eggs or olives. They're enjoyed as a snack or main dish, often accompanied by a glass of wine.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 100 g green olives, sliced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 empanada dough discs

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Season with paprika, cumin, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Remove from heat and let the filling cool. Add chopped hard-boiled eggs and olives.
  5. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each dough disc. Fold and crimp the edges to seal.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
