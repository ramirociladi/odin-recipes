Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Sandwich

Description

The bacon egg and cheese sandwich is the quintessential New York-style breakfast. Quite simply, it includes crispy bacon, fried eggs, yellow American cheese, all piled onto a poppy seed sandwich roll.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 4 teaspoons butter, divided
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices bread
