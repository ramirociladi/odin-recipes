Chimichurri Sauce

Description:

Chimichurri is a vibrant, herb-based sauce that accompanies grilled meats, especially asado. It has a fresh and tangy flavor from parsley, garlic, vinegar, and chili.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp oregano (fresh or dried)
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine the parsley, garlic, oregano, and chili flakes in a bowl.
  2. Add olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir to mix well.
  3. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes to develop flavors before serving.
